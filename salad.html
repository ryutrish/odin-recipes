Thai Mango Salad (Som Tum Mamuang)

Ingredients:

  • 1 large green mango, julienned
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons roasted peanuts, crushed
  • Juice of 2 limes (about 1/4 cup)
  • 1/2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp crushed chili pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk lime juice, soy sauce, honey, garlic, and chili pepper together.
  2. Combine Ingredients: In a large bowl, mix the julienned mango, carrot, bell pepper, and cilantro.
  3. Add Dressing: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well.
  4. Garnish: Top with crushed peanuts before serving.
  5. Serve: Enjoy this refreshing salad as a side or light meal!
