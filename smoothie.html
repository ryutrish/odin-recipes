Fruit Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana (frozen for extra creaminess)
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup orange juice (or milk of choice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • A few fresh mint leaves

Ingredients:

Instructions:

  • In a blender,blend the banana,frozen mixed berries,greek yogurt,orange juice (or milk).Add chia seeds.
  • Honey or maple syrup for extra sweetness
  • Taste the smoothie and adjust sweetness or liquid consistency as needed. Pour into a glass and garnish with fresh mint leaves.

Tips:

  • For a thicker smoothie, use more frozen fruits.
  • Add a scoop of protein powder for an extra boost.
